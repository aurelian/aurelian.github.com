Ai ridicat o "spranceana(Creating a weblog in 15 minutes)":http://media.rubyonrails.org/video/rails_take2_with_sound.mov, ti-a starnit "interesul(Snakes & Rubies: Pursuit of Beauty)":http://media.rubyonrails.org/presentations/pursuitofbeauty.pdf si "ai incercat(Rolling with Ruby on Rails)":http://www.onlamp.com/pub/a/onlamp/2005/01/20/rails.html.

Ai citit "articole(Planet RubyOnRails)":http://www.planetrubyonrails.org/, ai "cautat(Google)":http://google.com alte "tutoriale(Top 30 Ruby on Rails Tutorials)":http://www.econsultant.com/web-developer/ruby-rails-tutorials/ si ai cumparat "carti(Agile Web Development with Rails)":http://www.pragmaticprogrammer.com/titles/rails2/index.html.

Ti-a placut si ai inceput un "proiect(Marks: my Rails pet project)":http://locknet.ro/archive/2006-09-20-marks-my-rails-pet-project.html de la zero.

Atunci a aparut si prima problema: *nu stiai Ruby* la un nivel acceptabil. Si cum trebuia sa ai un raspuns pregatit pentru aceasta, te-ai intors la "articole(Planet Ruby!)":http://planetruby.0x42.net/, "tutoriale(why's - poignant - guide to ruby)":http://web.archive.org/web/20060615001901/http://www.poignantguide.net/ruby/ si "carti(Programming Ruby - The Pragmatic Programmer's Guide, 2-nd Edition)":http://www.pragmaticprogrammer.com/titles/ruby/index.html.

Te-ai "decis(Adio php, hello Ruby! Adio frustrari, hello happiness!)":http://locknet.ro/archive/2006-07-13-o-noua-palarie-fixa.html sa faci pasul cel mare, si sa lucrezi full-time cu o tehnologie ce iti permite sa iti imbunatatesti performantele de programator.
