Medick is an open-source web framework written in PHP.



Started in June 2005 at "berlios.de":http://developer.berlios.de/projects/medick/, Medick is at version 0.3.0pre2.

The website is "here":http://medick.locknet.ro/ and it includes a nice "FAQ":http://medick.locknet.ro/faq.html and the "API docs":http://medick.locknet.ro/api/.
